Rijksmuseum

The Rijksmuseum shop is the online gift shop of the Rijksmuseum Amsterdam.

The Rijksmuseum tells the story of 800 years of Dutch history, from 1200 to the present. This history is reflected in the works of world-famous artists and in various temporary exhibitions. In the Rijksmuseum shop, you can buy products inspired by these world-famous artists and masterpieces from the Rijksmuseum collection in Amsterdam.
